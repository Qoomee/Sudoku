Super Sudoku 16*16

Challenge your mind with this enhanced version of Sudoku! In this 16*16 super Sudoku, numbers 10-16 are represented by letters A-G, offering a more challenging gaming experience.

About The Game

Welcome to Super Sudoku 16*16, an enhanced version of the classic Sudoku game that expands the traditional 9*9 grid to a more challenging 16*16 format. In this version, numbers 10-16 are represented by letters A-G, adding an extra layer of complexity and excitement to the game.

Game Features

16*16 Grid

Experience a larger-scale Sudoku challenge in a 16*16 grid that will test your logical thinking skills.

Letter-Number System

Numbers 10-16 are represented by letters A-G, creating a unique and engaging gameplay experience.

Multiple Difficulty Levels

Choose from easy, medium, and hard difficulty levels to match your skill and experience.

How to Play

Fill in the 16*16 grid with numbers 1-9 and letters A-G, ensuring that each row, column, and 4*4 region contains no duplicates. Numbers 10-16 are represented by letters A-G, adding an extra challenge to the game. Use your logical thinking to complete this enhanced Sudoku challenge!

System Requirements

Super Sudoku 16*16 is a browser-based game that works on all modern devices. For the best experience, we recommend using an updated version of Chrome, Firefox, or Safari. The game is fully optimized for both desktop and mobile play.
